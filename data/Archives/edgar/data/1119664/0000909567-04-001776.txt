<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Nortel Provides Status Update</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">TORONTO &#150; Nortel Networks* Corporation &#091;NYSE/TSX:NT&#093; and its principal operating subsidiary,
Nortel Networks Limited (&#147;NNL&#148;), today provided a status update pursuant to the alternative
information guidelines of the Ontario Securities Commission. These guidelines contemplate that the
Company and NNL will normally provide bi-weekly updates on their affairs until such time as they
are current with their filing obligations under Canadian securities laws.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The Company and NNL reported that there have been no material developments in the matters reported
in their status updates of June&nbsp;2, 2004, June&nbsp;15, 2004, June&nbsp;29, 2004, July&nbsp;13, 2004, July&nbsp;27,
2004, August&nbsp;10, 2004, August&nbsp;19, 2004, September&nbsp;2, 2004, September&nbsp;16, 2004, September&nbsp;30, 2004,
October&nbsp;14, 2004, October&nbsp;27, 2004, November&nbsp;11, 2004, November&nbsp;24, 2004 and December&nbsp;8, 2004; the
Company&#146;s press release &#147;Nortel Networks Announces New Waiver from Export Development Canada&#148; dated
December&nbsp;10, 2004; and the Company&#146;s press release &#147;Nortel Provides Estimated Unaudited Results for
Q1, Q2 and Q3 2004 and Years 2001, 2002 and 2003&#148; dated December&nbsp;14, 2004, with the exception of
the matters described below.



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><U><B>Annual
Shareholders&#146; Meeting</B></U>

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The Company today was granted an order by the Ontario Superior Court of Justice (the &#147;Court&#148;)
extending the time for calling the Company&#146;s 2004 Annual Shareholders&#146; Meeting (the &#147;Meeting&#148;) to a
date no later than May&nbsp;31, 2005.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The Company had previously obtained an earlier order from the Court extending the time for calling
the Meeting to a date no later than March&nbsp;31, 2005 so that the Company&#146;s 2003 audited financial
statements could be completed and mailed to shareholders in time for the Meeting. The latest
extension was obtained, after discussions with the Staff of the United States Securities and
