<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:italic;">Performance Vesting
Restricted Stock Unit Awards</font></i></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December 16, 2004, the Committee approved awards of performance
vesting restricted stock units (&#147;RSUs&#148;) made to executive officers of the
Company pursuant to the terms of the Avaya Inc. 2004 Long Term Incentive
Plan.&#160; The RSUs will vest upon the date
the Committee determines that the Company&#146;s diluted earnings per share for the
fiscal year ended September 30, 2007 is at least equal to a specified target;
provided, however, that the percentage of RSUs that may vest shall be
determined based on a schedule to be attached to each RSU agreement; provided,
further, however, that if the aggregate earnings per share total calculated by
combining the Company&#146;s diluted earnings per share for each of the fiscal years
ended September 30, 2005, 2006 and 2007 is less than a specified target, then
all RSUs shall be forfeited and cancelled.&#160;
Attached as Exhibit 10.1 is the form of Avaya Inc. 2004 Long Term
Incentive Plan Performance Vesting Restricted Stock Unit Award Agreement.&#160; However, information regarding earnings per
share and other targets have been omitted as they involve confidential business
information, the disclosure of which would have an adverse effect on the
Company.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:italic;">Tenovis Special Incentive
Plan</font></i></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December 16, 2004, the
Compensation Committee (the &#147;Committee&#148;) of the Board of Directors of Avaya
Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) approved a special incentive plan designed to promote a
successful integration of the recent acquisition of Tenovis Germany GmbH and
its subsidiaries (collectively, &#147;Tenovis&#148;) and the achievement of three
metrics:&#160; targeted revenue for Germany,
targeted revenue for the EMEA region, and contribution margin for the EMEA
region for the calendar year 2005.&#160; EMEA
consists of the European, Middle Eastern and African operations of the Company.&#160; Participants in the plan include certain
employees of the Company involved in the integration and management of Tenovis,
including executive officers of the Company.&#160;
Under the arrangement, the aggregate amount available for all awards to
eligible participants, including executive officers, is not expected to be
material to the </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Company.&#160; Because the plan does not exist in any formal
document, only a written description thereof has been included with this Form
8-K as Exhibit 10.2.&#160; However,
information regarding the target metrics has been omitted as it involves
confidential business information, the disclosure of which would have an
adverse effect on the Company.</font></p>
