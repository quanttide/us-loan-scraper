Item 8.01. Other Events.

     On December 20, 2004, Ardent Health Services LLC (the “Registrant”), announced that its subsidiary, Ardent Health Services, Inc. (the “Company”), has commenced the solicitation of consents from the holders of its 10% Senior Subordinated Notes due 2013 to an amendment to the financial reporting covenant contained in the indenture governing the Notes. The amendment would provide that the Registrant is not required to comply with the covenant until May 2, 2005. In addition, the Company is seeking a waiver of all existing defaults under the indenture with respect to the financial reporting covenant.

     A copy of the Registrant’s press release is filed as an exhibit to this Form 8-K and is incorporated herein by reference.
