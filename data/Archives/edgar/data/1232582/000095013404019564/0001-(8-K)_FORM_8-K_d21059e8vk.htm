ITEM 7.01 REGULATION FD DISCLOSURE

On December 17, 2004, Ashford Hospitality Trust, Inc. (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing that its Board of Directors declared a dividend for its fourth quarter ended December 31, 2004, of approximately $0.5878 per fully-diluted share, for the Company’s 8.55% Series A Cumulative Preferred Stock. The dividend is payable January 18, 2005, for shareholders of record on December 31, 2004. The total dividend reflects a partial dividend of $0.0534 per fully-diluted share for the period from September 22 to September 30, 2004, and the regular quarterly preferred dividend of $0.5344 per fully-diluted share. A copy of the press release is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.49 and is incorporated herein by reference.
