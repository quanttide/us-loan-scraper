Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

 

On December 17, 2004, Trump Hotels & Casino Resorts, Inc. (the “Company”), Trump Atlantic City Associates and Trump Casino Holdings, LLC entered into that certain commitment letter (the “Commitment Letter”) with Morgan Stanley Senior Funding, Inc. (“Morgan Stanley”) and UBS Loan Finance LLC and UBS Securities LLC (together, “UBS”), pursuant to which Morgan Stanley and UBS have agreed to provide a financing commitment for, and act as joint lead arrangers and bookrunners in connection with, a $500 million working capital facility to be entered into by the Company upon the effective date of the Company’s plan of reorganization (the “Plan”) under chapter 11 of the United States Bankruptcy Code. Morgan Stanley would also act as administrative agent for the working capital facility. The Commitment Letter, which is subject to approval of the United States Bankruptcy Court for the district of New Jersey (the “Court”), is attached as Exhibit 10.1 hereto.

 

Item 8.01. Other Events.

 

On December 15, 2004, the Company and certain of its subsidiaries (collectively, the “Debtors”) filed the Plan and the accompanying disclosure statement (the “Disclosure Statement”) with the Court. The Plan and Disclosure Statement, as well as the Commitment Letter described in Item 1.01, implement certain terms of that certain restructuring support agreement, dated as of October 20, 2004, among certain of the Debtors, Donald J. Trump and certain holders of the Debtors’ public indebtedness, as previously disclosed in the Company’s Current Report on Form 8-K filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (the “Commission”) on October 21, 2004. The Plan and Disclosure Statement are attached hereto as Exhibits 2.1 and 2.2, respectively. The Court has set a January 21, 2005 hearing date to evaluate the adequacy of the Disclosure Statement. Further information concerning the Company’s chapter 11 cases, as well as the documents filed with the Court, are available on the Debtors’ website at www.THCRrecap.com.

 

The descriptions of the Commitment Letter, Plan and Disclosure Statement and the transactions contemplated thereby are qualified in their entirety by reference to such documents.

 

Any securities proposed to be issued in connection with the Plan and Disclosure Statement have not been registered under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, or any state securities laws and unless so registered may not be offered or sold in the United States except pursuant to an exemption from, or in a transaction not subject to, the registration requirements of the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, and applicable state securities laws.
