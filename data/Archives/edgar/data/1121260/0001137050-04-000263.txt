<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-right:72pt; margin-bottom:0pt; width:540pt; font-family:Courier New;font-size:10pt"><BR></P>

ds. </P>

Clarendon Avenue, Phoenix, Arizona 85016, (&#147;Clancy and Co.&#148;) &nbsp;independent auditors of the Company for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2004. &nbsp;</P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-right:72pt; margin-bottom:0pt; width:540pt; font-family:Courier New;font-size:10pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-right:72pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:36pt; width:540pt; line-height:11pt; font-family:Courier New;font-size:10pt">Prior to the engagement of Clancy and Co., neither the Company nor anyone on its behalf consulted with such firm regarding the application of accounting principles to a specified transaction whether completed or uncompleted, the type of audit opinion that might be rendered on the Company&#146;s financial statements or as to any matter that was either the subject of a disagreement with the previous independent auditor or was a reportable event. &nbsp;The decision to change accountants was recommended and approved by the Company&#146;s Board of Directors.</P>
